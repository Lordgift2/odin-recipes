Nigeria Suya

Finished Dish Of Nigeria suya

Suya is a Nigeria street food at its finest, Skewers of spiced, nutty,smoky,charred beef served with raw onion,tomatoes,cilanto, lettuce and freshly squzeezed lime juice which could be accompany with chilled fruit juice

Ingredient for the Nigeria Suya

  • 1 table spoon finely grounded roasted peanut
  • 1 teaspoon grounded cayenna pepper
  • 1 teaspoon grounded paparika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon grounded ginger
  • ½ teaspoon galic powder
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • 1½ pounds of tri-tip stick,cut into bite-size pieceies
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into bite-size pieces
  • ¼ onion, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 4 mushrooms, halved

Directions in preparing Suya

  1. Mix ground peanuts, cayenne pepper, paprika, salt, ginger, garlic powder, and onion powder together in a bowl
  2. Combine spice mixture and beef in a resealable plastic bag; shake until well coated. Marinate in the refrigerator, about 30 minutes
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate
  4. Place beef, bell pepper, onion, and mushrooms in alternating order onto skewers.
  5. Cook on the preheated grill, flipping once, until meat is browned and vegetables are soft, 10 to 15 minutes.
